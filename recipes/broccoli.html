Broccoli

Description

Green and a good vegetable.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Heat wok
  2. Add oil
  3. Add garlic
  4. Add broccoli
  5. Stir them around for a bit and make them dance
  6. Add salt and stir more
  7. Add water and cover with lid
  8. Let them simmer for like 2 minutes
  9. Serve!
  10. Realize that they were still not fully cooked .-.
  11. Put them back in the wok
  12. They're burnt now :(
  13. Eat and hope you don't die
