Stir-Fried Pasta With a Lot of Vegetables

Description

Pasta with vegetable!!! Filling and nutritious!!!

Ingredients

  • Pasta (as much as you want, I have yet to cook the perfect amount for myself it's always either too much or too little)
  • Vegetables that you like! Recipe will use:
    • Broccoli
    • Onion
    • Mushroom
  • Salt
  • Soy sauce
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Boil water in pot
  2. While waiting for water to boil, cut all vegetables to biteable sizes
  3. Heat wok
  4. Add oil
  5. Water is probably boiling by now, add pasta to pot and cook for like 10 mins
  6. Put onion and mushroom in wok
  7. Stir stir
  8. Add broccoli and stir stir
  9. Add salt and stir stir
  10. Simmer time, add water and put that lid on
  11. Stir the pasta every so often whenever you feel like it
  12. Pasta is probably cooked now, take out and toss in oil
  13. Add pasta to stir fried veggies
  14. Toss and stir and add soy sauce
  15. Hope the pasta doesn't stick to bottom of wok :(
  16. Serve and hope it doesn't taste like dish soap again
