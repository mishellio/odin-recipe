Tomato Egg

Description

Egg with nice flavour! Not supposed to be super watery.

Ingredients

  • 2 egg
  • 1 tomato
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Crack eggs in bowl and beat them up, set aside
  2. Chop chop tomato and set aside
  3. Heat wok
  4. Add oil
  5. Pour egg in
  6. Cook till nicely cooked, split to smaller sizes
  7. Add tomato, stir stir
  8. Add salt, stir stir
  9. Attempt to flatten tomatoes so egg can have more tomato flavour
  10. Serve with rice or something, have good noms
