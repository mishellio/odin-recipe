Eggplant Fried Rice

Description

Eggplant fried rice mmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmm

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Eggplant
  • Meat or some sort
  • Green onion
  • Salt
  • Soy sauce
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Cook rice at quick cook because you are really craving eggplant fried rice
  2. Add too much water because you realize that you forgot how to cook rice and fail your asian ancestors
  3. Have mushy rice
  4. Chop eggplant to small small pieces, chop meat and green onion to less small pieces
  5. Heat wok add oil
  6. Add eggplant and meat
  7. Add salt and soy sauce and stir stir
  8. Add rice and green onion
  9. Rice stick to bottom of wok :(
  10. Add salt and soy sauce again while you scrape the rice off the bottom of the wok
  11. Serve and eat your mushy rice
  12. Try again next time
